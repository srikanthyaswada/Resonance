Why Resonance ?

Education System

Every part of the Resonance system - be it academics, studentfaculty relation, performance monitoring, orientation, curriculum execution or other relevant areas - follow a set of do's and don'ts along with target based goals. This ensures that every student at Resonance experiences uniform attention and interaction with faculty members along with setting their individual goals and achievements.

Experienced Faculty

A team of India's finest faculty members is the core of Resonance experience. Many of our faculty members for Pre-Engineering division are IITians/NITians and Doctors from the top Medical Colleges of India for Medical Division, CA/ CS/ MBAs/Law Graduates for Commerce & Law Program Division. All the faculty members are recruited through a rigorous recruitment process which involves scrutiny of applications, written test, demonstration lecture and a personal interview. New/ fresh recruited faculty members undergo an intensive training program called Faculty Training Program (FTP) where his/her knowledge, attitude and communication skills are further sharpened. New faculty members also attend the classes of senior faculty members to understand the teaching methodology, effective use of teaching aids and general behaviour in classroom. To further groom and refine the skills of young teachers, a Faculty Development Program (FDP) is designed under which continuous enrichment and value addition is done to keep them aware of changing trends in competitive examinations. Challenger Test Series (CTS) is organized for the faculty members to rejuvenate their motivation level and enhance the skills of solving questions within stipulated time with speed and accuracy.
